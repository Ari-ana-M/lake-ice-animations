Lake Ice Study – Overview

Supplementary materials for “Assessment of ERA5-Land Lake Ice Related Variables from Satellite Observations,” Ariana Mansingh, M.Sc. Thesis.

These results illustrate the spatial and temporal differences in freeze-up and break-up between IMS and ERA5-Land. For the northern lakes (Great Bear Lake, Great Slave Lake, Lake Athabasca, and Lake Winnipeg), the freeze-up and break-up periods were derived through comparison of ERA5-Land lake ice fraction with CIS observations. For the Laurentian Great Lakes, these periods were determined through comparison of ERA5-Land lake ice fraction with NOAA ice fraction.
